AcadNet is a peer-led academic collaboration platform designed to help students connect, share resources, and support each other's learning journeys. Join study groups, access shared resources, and participate in Q&A forums to enhance your academic experience.

Features

AcadNet offers a suite of features designed to enhance your learning experience and foster collaboration among peers.

groups

Study Group Management

Easily create, join, and manage study groups tailored to your courses and interests.

folder_shared

Resource Sharing

Share and access notes, study guides, and past papers with your peers.

chat

Real-Time Discussion

Engage in real-time discussions, ask questions, and exchange ideas.

recommend

Recommendations

Get personalized recommendations for study groups and resources.

How It Works

Getting started with AcadNet is simple. Follow these three easy steps:

1

Join a Group

Find study groups that match your courses or create your own.

2

Share/Explore Resources

Access a wealth of resources shared by your peers or contribute your own.

3

Collaborate and Grow

Engage in discussions, ask questions, and support each other's learning.
